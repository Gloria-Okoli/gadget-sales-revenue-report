# 📘 Gadget Sales & Revenue Report

An interactive Power BI report analyzing gadget sales performance
and revenue trends — built during Data Analyst training at
Tech Studio Academy (TS Academy).

---

## 📌 Overview

This report examines gadget sales data across products and time
periods to uncover revenue patterns, identify top-selling items,
and support strategic business decisions through visual storytelling.

## 💡 Key Insights

- Analyzed sales data across **250 customers** with **3M total revenue**
- **Apple** emerged as the top-performing brand with 197K profit
- **Medium income** customers generated the highest profit at 347K
- **White** colored gadgets led total purchases at 39.22%
- Monthly revenue peaked in **March at 1,047K** after a February dip
- **Electronics and Accessories** tracked across North, South, East and West regions
